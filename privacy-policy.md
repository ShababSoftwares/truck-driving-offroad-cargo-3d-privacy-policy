# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

Muhammad Arif Shabab ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Truck Driving Offroad Cargo 3D mobile game (the "Service").

**We do not show ads and we do not collect personal data from you through the app.** The game is designed to run without requiring data permissions for personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

- **No account required:** The App does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your email address, username, or similar account credentials through the App for registration purposes.

- **Game progress and preferences:** Data you generate in the Game may include:
  - Game progress and level completion status
  - Gameplay settings and control preferences (for example sensitivity, camera, audio)

  Much of this information is stored locally on your device (see Data Storage and Security).

### Device Information

- **No advertising identifiers:** We do not use advertising identifiers (such as AAID/IDFA) because the App does not show ads.
- **No third-party analytics SDKs:** We do not integrate third-party analytics SDKs for tracking.

### Automatically Collected Information

**Log data:** We do not operate our own backend servers and we do not collect log data (such as IP address) from you through the App for tracking purposes.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for various purposes:

- **To provide and maintain our Service:** Including running the game, enabling gameplay features, and delivering core functionality.

- **To remember your preferences:** Such as gameplay settings stored on your device.

- **To provide customer support:** We may use information you voluntarily provide when you contact us to respond to inquiries.

- **To improve our Service:** Including understanding reliability issues and improving features, subject to applicable settings and law.

- **To ensure security:** To help detect abuse or technical problems, including through information processed by trusted service providers where applicable.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- Your game progress and gameplay settings are primarily stored locally on your device using app storage mechanisms.

- We rely on your device's platform security features to protect data at rest on the device.

### Cloud Storage (if applicable)

- The App is designed to work without a mandatory cloud account for core features. If we introduce optional cloud sync or similar features in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and describe what is stored, where, and how it is protected (for example encryption in transit using SSL/TLS).

### Data Retention

- Data stored only on your device remains until you clear app data, uninstall the App, or remove items from your photo library.

- We do not store your personal data on our servers.

### Communication Preferences

- You can adjust notification-related preferences in the App where such features exist.

### Data Portability
